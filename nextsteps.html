Getting a positive pregnancy test result may cause you to have various emotions which is completely normal. Whether you feel a bit down, anxious, or are excited about your positive pregnancy result, it's important to just take a few deep breaths to keep yourself together.

  2. Take a home pregnancy test, following the instructions carefully.
  3. Make an appointment with your doctor.
  4. Confirm your pregnancy, home pregnancy tests can give false results.
  5. Even if your pregnancy is a surprise, DON'T PANIC and let it sink in.

YOU DECIDE NOT TO CONTINUE YOUR PREGNANCY

You can end your pregnancy by either choosing surgical abortion or medical abortion. Considering an abortion may cause many questions to arise and there are a lot of resources available to help you decide what's best for you.

If you are feeling unsure, you might ask yourself:

+ Are you ready to become a parent?

+ How would your future be affected if you decide to have a baby at this point in your life?

+ Is adoption an option for you?

+ How would having child affect your family?

+ Are you strongly against abortion for either religious or personal reasons>

+ What support would you need if you decide to keep it?
